Car Key Replacement

If you lose your car keys or they get stolen, a locksmith can help replace them. They can create new keys based on your car's specifications, including both mechanical keys and electronic transponder keys.

Key Programming

For modern cars with electronic transponder keys or key fobs, locksmiths can program new keys to work with your vehicle's immobilizer system. This process involves syncing the transponder or key fob with your car's security system to enable proper functionality.
